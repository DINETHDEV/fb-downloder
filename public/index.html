How to Download Facebook Videos

FB Downloader is a free online tool that lets you save any public Facebook video directly to your device in HD or SD quality — no app installation needed.

1

Copy the Video Link

Open Facebook, find the video you want, click the three dots (⋯) menu, and select "Copy link".
